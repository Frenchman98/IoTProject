Track your UV Exposure

While exercising outside can be a blast, skin cancer is not. And the more time you spend in the sun, the greater your risk. Always wear proper sun protection.

Additionally, use our device, and you will get an alert when you have reached a set UV intake for the day.

Note: our devices alone cannot prevent skin cancer. See your doctor if any unusual skin conditions develop, and limit your UV exposure. SunRunr is not liable for anything other than being awesome

Change your devices: anytime

It happens: you either lose your device or damage it, and now you need to update your device. Or perhaps, you have received a second device to keep with you on the go.

Adding a new device is easy: just visit our "Add a Device" page on the navigation bar.

The SunRunr application is a web application for monitoring your outdoor fitness activities, sun exposure, and weather conditions during walking, running, and biking.
